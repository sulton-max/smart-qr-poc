# v0.3 — Accounts & ownership

*Last updated: 2026-06-19*

**Status:** 🚧 In Progress · **Started:** 2026-06-19 · **Completed:** —

> The deliverable half of cycle 2 (two-cycle model → `wow-two-ws/conventions/development/dev-cycle.md`): a
> registered account on top of the guest-first identity — sign in with Google, claim the codes you made as a
> guest, and reach them from any device. Auth infra is built **inline** here; the reusable pieces extract to the
> backend SDK in v0.4 (the extraction half).

### Iteration 1 — Sign in: authenticate with a Google account

- [x] Ability to sign in with a Google account.
- [x] Ability to stay signed in across visits and to sign out.

### Iteration 2 — Claim: keep your guest codes when you sign in

- [x] Ability to keep the codes you created as a guest once you sign in.

### Iteration 3 — Cross-device: reach your codes from any device

- [x] Ability to manage your codes from any device once signed in.

### Iteration 4 — Verification: sign-in, claim, and cross-device end to end

- [ ] Run the E2E suite → sign-in, claim, and cross-device scenarios green (Google verification stubbed).
- [ ] Sign in with Google in a fresh browser → land in the app as a registered user with name and email shown.
- [ ] Create codes as a guest, then sign in → those codes stay listed under the account.
- [ ] Sign in with the same account in a second browser → the same codes appear.
- [ ] Sign out → the app returns to the guest or anonymous state.
