## Positioning v2 ‚Äî free generator + the forwarder

- **Front door = a free QR generator, any style** (logo ¬∑ colors ¬∑ shapes ¬∑ frames). Generation is Tier-0 (~free) ‚Üí give it **all** away ‚Üí top-of-funnel + SEO magnet.
- **Monetize the redirect** ‚Äî dynamic codes (one code ‚Üí many destinations, never-expire) are the only part with ongoing cost + durable value ‚Üí the paid layer **and** the wedge.
- one-liner: *"Make any QR, free. Pay only when you want it to forward ‚Äî and never expire."*

## Pricing experiment ‚Äî the $1 entry

- **Free:** unlimited **static** generation, any style, export SVG/PNG/PDF (the magnet).
- **Paid (candidate):** dynamic/forwarding codes above a small free limit ‚Äî sell *cheap dynamic + never-expire*.
- **Price catch:** $1/mo is a great hook but **monthly $1 loses ~33% to Stripe** (`$0.30 + 2.9%`) ‚Üí bill it **annually ($12/yr)** or as a founder price; keep a higher Pro/API tier for ARPU.
- **Why low works:** kills the price objection ‚Üí lifts trial‚Üípaid; undercuts incumbents' $5-30 + never-expire.
- **Risk:** "toy" signal ¬∑ churny low-LTV users ¬∑ support underwater at $1 margin.
- **Test:** $1/mo-annual vs $3/mo on **retained revenue** (conversion √ó price √ó retention), not conversion alone.
